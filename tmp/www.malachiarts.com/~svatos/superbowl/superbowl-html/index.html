State issued placards or license plates must accompany all accessible parking permits.

RV parking is not allowed in NFL Controlled lots. A list of RV locations will be posted here soon.
All Super Bowl XLII parking permit orders will be shipped via FedEx. FedEx cannot be delivered to Post Office boxes. Orders placed prior to January 7th may take up to 5 business days to process and ship. Beginning January 8th all orders will be processed and shipped within 48 hours. A strict anti-scalping policy is in effect for Super Bowl XLII and all orders are reviewed by the NFL. Any questions regarding shipment of orders please call 866-330-PARK.
